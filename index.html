Inside the Prevention Checklist

Compliance & Paperwork

Learn how to create a custom Workplace Violence Prevention Plan (WVPP) and maintain legally compliant incident logs to cover your assets.

Architectural Security

Discover how to "design out the danger" using uniform lighting, clear sight-lines, and strict access control to sensitive employee-only zones.

Spotting Red Flags

Train your managers to identify "Level One" warning signs, from severe mood swings to sudden, unexplained disregard for basic safety rules.

De-Escalation & Verbal Judo

Equip your team with basic de-escalation body language, safe distancing techniques, and the verbal skills needed to defuse situations safely.

Safe Reporting Systems

Implement zero-retaliation reporting channels and ensure Support Systems like your EAP and the 988 Crisis Lifeline are properly utilized.

Training & Documentation

Move beyond dusty manuals with interactive hazard recognition education and meticulous record-keeping protocols that actually work.
